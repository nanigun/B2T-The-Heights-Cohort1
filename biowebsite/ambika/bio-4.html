I'm a New York Yankees fan

I'm not into too many sports. I enjoy swimming and cycling. But I do love to watch the Yankees play and who doesn't love Aaron Judge. This young man is bringing a lot of interest to the game from his powerful hits. Let's hope he will be a Yankee till he retires.


Picnics on Govenor's Island

If you've never been to Governor's Island, plan a trip. For cheapskates like me, plan your picnic on a weekend so the ferry ride over is free. On weekdays, there is a fee. With over 170 acres, there is plenty to explore. It's my idea of a great summer spot. My husband and I bring our bikes over and a picnic basket and we're good for at least half the day.
